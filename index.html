About me

I am a Computer Science Engineer. I love to learn new things and, that's why I have interdisciplinary skills. I have also organized, volunteered, and hosted several events as an IEEE Member and Google Developer Student Club Member.

As an intern, I have lead designers for a startup company instatees.in. I have also, developed a mini- game for Children ,published in playstore as Bird Blash under my startup Exult Developers.

During my Higher Secondary Schooling, i was interested in Designing and Won Web Design competitions in District Level and this made me a tech enthusiast and a wish to learn more about computer Science. I am Maker as well. I enjoy making DIY projects. In my childhood, I used to make stuff I see in DIY shows shown on TV. Later I made a DIY tesla coil with just a MOSFET, 12v battery, some copper wires, and aluminum foil. During that time, I was inspired by the works of Nikola Tesla which led me to take Engineering as my degree.

I used to help my father in managing and coordinate works during construction of my house, i wish to know and learn more about buildings and its construction. So i enrolled in Minor B-tech in Civil Engineering along with my B-tech in Computer Science and Engineering.

Currently, my focus is on Ai based . have enrolled in various courses on QuickLab , Coursera and looking for opportunities to contribute to the Cloud Based Projects.
